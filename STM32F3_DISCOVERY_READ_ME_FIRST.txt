STM32F3-Discovery board

Note:
If Serial Wire Viewer (SWV) shall be used the solder bridge SB10 (SWO) needs 
to be soldered (ON). When SB10 is soldered the MCU PB3 pin (SWO) connects to 
ST-LINK and SWV can be used.  

More information is available in the STM32F3-Discovery User Manual (UM1570)
http://www.st.com/st-web-ui/static/active/en/resource/technical/document/user_manual/DM00063382.pdf?s_searchtype=keyword
